The Infinitus Framework

July 28, 2025

The Infinitus Framework presents a revolutionary conceptual architecture for developing self-improving, autonomous multi-agent AI systems capable of continuous operation with theoretical infinite context capabilities. This groundbreaking research combines Orchestrator-Executor models, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architectures, and continual learning mechanisms for fully autonomous private deployment.

The framework addresses critical challenges in autonomous AI development including error propagation management, security vulnerabilities from data poisoning attacks, and computational overhead optimization. Advanced Explainable AI (XAI) integration ensures transparency and accountability in autonomous decision-making processes.

Applications span from revolutionary scientific discovery capabilities to fully autonomous business operations, while addressing essential ethical considerations including accountability frameworks, bias prevention, and potential job displacement impacts. The research provides comprehensive governance frameworks for responsible autonomous AI deployment.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Orchestrator-Executor architecture and infinite context management
  • Advanced RAG implementation and continual learning systems
  • Critical risk mitigation and security frameworks
  • Explainable AI integration and transparency mechanisms
  • Applications, ethics, and governance considerations
